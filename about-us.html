Our story

We begin this journey 5 years ago with the dream of bringing the best bread to our community. Our founder, Cecilia Rios has always had a passing for baking, and she decided to turn her passion into a business. With the help of her family, she opened Panaderia Buen Pan, a small bakery on the corner of her house.
